"Algorithmic" internships

These internship are focussed on algorithms, software and/or control.

Building infinite maps

Investigate nonparametric Bayesian methods, while acquainting yourself with different models from the Hidden Markov Model to the Chinese restaurant process! (Read more)

Compressed sensing

Sparse coding is currently a very hot research topic in image reconstruction, deblurring, MRI, EEG, etc. You get the chance to investigate sparsity in sensor data for robots and smartphones at DoBots! (Read more)

Vision-based spatial awareness

Devise a method that can extract features from video that are important for localization; in particular large planes such as the ground plane, walls and ceilings! (Read more)

"Technological" internships

These internship are focussed on full solutions, hardware development, and embedded programming.

3D printing of phone bodies

Work on a robotic body for a smartphone which can be used for multi-robot demos and experiments! (Read more)

First aid quadcopter

Work on a hardware prototype of the payload using a commercial-off-the-shelf quadcopter such as the Parrot and enable it to reliably fly towards a person in need and come back! (Read more)

Design a farm land robot

Find out what farmers need and define the requirements of the robot, based on an application in agriculture like inspecting crops for insects or certain colors! (Read more)
