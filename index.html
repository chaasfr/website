DoBots*

*It is in the name.

Groups of bots searching, cooperating, grazing and negotiating. DoBots are modeled after nature where groups of animals, colonies, flocks and swarms show intelligent behaviour.

Boost Intelligence

DoBots envisions a world of connected robots in order to improve the quality of our life

DoBots provides the software and IT infrastructure for robot autonomy & intelligence to make that world reality.

  • Smart Homes

    “To convert homes into living systems, in which all single parts cooperate to increase the comfort we experience at home.” That’s the goal of DoBots. Therefore DoBots introduced wireless screw terminals that makes everything controllable!

    “Think of vacuum cleaners that can decide for themselves when to go about the house: not just for cleaning, but perhaps also to check if everything is in order... and notifying the police when something is wrong.”

  • SmartBots

    A phone with wheels, a spoon with a drive, keys that whistle... The only thing that is missing is the robot brain. DoBots works on this challenge by developing and providing software and IT infrastructures for robot autonomy & intelligence.

    Dobots aims to improve the quality of our life. Therefore devices should be able to help us better and better. This will become easier the more devices are attached to the internet. Hence, smartphone-powered devices are interesting to us. Not in the first place because they can be turned on by you and me, but especially because they can then be turned on by our robots. Thus our robots will need to know us to be really good personal assistants
